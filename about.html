About Crescent Stars Cricket Club

Crescent Stars Cricket Club is a passionate community cricket team competing in the Calgary Cricket League. Founded to promote sportsmanship, skill development, and camaraderie, we take pride in representing our city and supporting cricket talent at all levels.

Our goal is to nurture local talent and bring competitive cricket to the forefront of Calgary's sporting scene.
